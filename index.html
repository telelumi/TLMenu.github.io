Features

What TLMenu brings

The complete feature set of the TLMenu Roblox script.

01

QuickActions on Players

Hug, Kiss, Slap, and over 40+ different animations, all applied directly to other players through the QuickActions bar.

02

German/French Voice Skin Stealer

Pulls German and French voice skins straight from other players.

03

Universal FE

Universal filtering-enabled support for maximum compatibility across different games.

04

Troll Options via QuickActions

A range of troll functions, instantly accessible right from the QuickActions bar.

05

Emote System

Quick access to the emote wheel with a single keypress — just hit "." (dot).

06

Recolorable GUI + Custom Cursor

A fully recolorable interface paired with its own custom cursor that matches your chosen theme.

07

SmartBar Tabs

Clean navigation across Home, Character, Scripts, Actions, Playerlist, and Settings.
